About Me

Hi! My name is Regan and I'm a web developer. I'm a lover of coffee, true crime documentaries, and coding. I found my passion for coding while I was in high school and I used the little skill I had at the time to help my friends design their blogs. Fast forward a few years to when I found the 1150 Academy and joined their .Net Development class to hone my skill and turn my hobby into a career.

I've been a creator all my life. From embroidery to pottery to painting and everything in between, I have always strived to create a beautiful product. I love coding because it allows me to keep creating beautiful, but highly functional products with real-world use. My goal in my work is to use the design-centric part of my mind in conjunction with my skill for developing to output something easy to use but also aesthetically pleasing to its users.
